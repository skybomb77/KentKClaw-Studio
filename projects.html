Studio projects

Two distinct products, one studio.

Kent & KClaw Studio is building two separate product tracks. ToneForge handles AI music generation for creators and brands. WireVision handles turning songs into short-form videos faster with beat sync and creator-first workflows.

AI music generation

ToneForge

An AI music tool for short videos, ad-ready soundtracks, and creator content. It is built for creators, editors, and brands that need usable music faster.

  • Generates music for content, ads, and campaigns
  • Built around short-form and commercial use cases
  • Separate product with its own positioning and landing page

Explore ToneForge

Audio to short video

WireVision

A short-form video creation product that turns songs into publishable videos with beat sync, templates, subtitle motion, and export presets.

  • Turns audio into short videos faster
  • Focused on TikTok, Reels, and Shorts workflows
  • Separate product track from ToneForge

Explore WireVision

Why this structure works better

Keeping these products separate makes the studio easier to understand. One product is about generating music. The other is about turning audio into short-form video. They can connect later, but they should not be explained as the same thing on the website.
