FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform.

What file formats do you support for upload?
We support MP3, WAV, FLAC for audio uploads, and PNG, JPG, WebP for image uploads. Export formats include MP3, WAV for audio and MP4 for video.
Can I use the generated content commercially?
Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include a full commercial license. Free plan content is for personal use only.
How long does generation take?
Most music tracks are generated in under 2 minutes. Video generation typically takes 3-5 minutes depending on length and complexity. Pro users get priority queue access.
Do I need to install any software?
No. Kent & KClaw Studio is fully cloud-based. Just open your browser, sign in, and start creating. Works on Mac, Windows, and Linux.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no hidden fees. Cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
What AI models power your engines?
Our music engine uses state-of-the-art audio synthesis models. For visuals, we run AI pipelines powered by Stable Diffusion and custom motion models on high-performance GPU hardware.
